# Update Learner Evidence

First read root AGENTS.md and docs/governance/PROGRESS_ADAPTATION.md in full. Remain in LEARNER_TUTOR mode unless only aggregating curriculum evidence. Do not edit learner source.

Use concrete module/test/benchmark/review/oral/journal evidence. Update demonstrated abilities separately from weaknesses. Every weakness records evidence, confidence, recurrence, proposed reinforcement, and revisit timing. Avoid personality, motivation, intelligence, or diligence judgments. Do not infer mastery from one pass or close a weakness without transfer evidence. Preserve contradictory evidence and lower confidence when appropriate.
