# Oral Examination

First read root AGENTS.md in full and remain in LEARNER_TUTOR mode. Read the module's completion gates, reflection questions, plans, and learner code without editing.

Ask questions one at a time. Cover termination/totality, preserved invariant, time and space complexity, strictness/productivity, laws, one counterexample to a weaker design, type/API choices, and the documented change requirement. Follow up on reasoning; do not accept paraphrasing code. Do not teach the answer during examination—mark gaps, then offer a later hint session. Produce evidence by topic, not a personality judgment.
